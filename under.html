WELLNESSGAUGE

Your health, your numbers, your choice

Focus on nutrient-dense foods

Include a variety of whole foods like lean meats, whole grains, healthy fats, dairy products, fruits, and vegetables. Ensure your diet provides adequate calories, vitamins, and minerals.

Eat more frequently

Have multiple small meals throughout the day. Include calorie-dense snacks like nuts, seeds, avocados, cheese, and dried fruits.

Engage in strength training

Exercises like weight lifting can help build muscle mass, which contributes to healthy weight gain. Focus on exercises that target large muscle groups.

Drink calorie-rich beverages

Choose smoothies, milkshakes, or other high-calorie drinks in addition to water. Avoid excessive consumption of sugary drinks, which can lead to unhealthy weight gain.

Have regular meals

Skipping meals can lead to insufficient calorie intake, preventing you from gaining weight. Make sure to have regular, well-balanced meals every day.

Seek medical advice:

If you're struggling to gain weight or maintain a healthy weight, consult a nutritionist or healthcare provider. They can help identify any underlying conditions affecting your weight and create a personalized nutrition plan.
