Eat a Balanced Diet

Focus on whole foods like fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid processed and high-sugar foods.

Stay Active Daily

Incorporate regular physical activities such as walking, jogging, or cycling. Strength training helps boost metabolism.

Practice Portion Control

Be mindful of how much you eat, even with healthy foods. Use smaller plates and avoid second helpings.

Drink Plenty of Water

Stay hydrated to avoid mistaking thirst for hunger. Replace sugary drinks with water or herbal tea.
